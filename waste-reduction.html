Waste Reduction: Master the 3Rs

Reducing waste helps keep land and water cleaner while saving energy and natural materials.

Focus on the "3Rs": Reduce first, then reuse, and recycle only when it's the right option. When fewer items end up in bins, there is less pollution and less demand for new resources.

♻️ The 3Rs Strategy for Zero Waste

  • Refuse - Say no to single-use items like bags, straws, and extra packaging.
  • Reuse - Find new purposes for items and repair things instead of discarding them.
  • Recycle - Learn your local rules and recycle paper, plastic, and metal correctly.
  • Compost - Turn food scraps into nutrient-rich soil for gardens and plants.
  • Donate - Give items you no longer need to others instead of throwing them away.
